Primate Cell Type Database

Intracellular recordings of non-human primates (NHP) cells sampled from the lateral prefrontal cortex. ~370 patch clamp recordings for 2 protocols: 1 second current pulse & 3 millisecond pulse. Morphology obtained by biocytin injections and confocal imaging at 63x magnification.
