The Primate Database Team

We are a conglomerate of two laboratories at the Robarts Reasearch Institute, Western University, Canada. We bring the technical strengths of both ourgroups to allow for the creation of this database.
Below are listed the PIs, and members categorized by roles.

Primary Investigators

  • Electrophysiology
    Patch clamp recordings for various stimulation protocols (i.e., long current pulse, 3ms pulse, ramp, etc.).
  • Morphology
    3-D morphological reconstructions, laminar organization, histoimmuno-staining images.
