Primate Cell Type Database

Intracellular recordings of non-human primates (NHP) cells sampled from the lateral prefrontal cortex. ~370 patch clamp recordings for 2 protocols: 1 second current pulse & 3 millisecond pulse. Morphology obtained by biocytin injections and confocal imaging at 63x magnification.

Ex vivo data 

We have a suite of experimental data generated recently in the lab including: electrophysiology, morphology, and molecular.

  • Electrophysiology
    Patch clamp recordings for various stimulation protocols (i.e., long current pulse, 3ms pulse, ramp, etc.).
  • Morphology
    3-D morphological reconstructions, laminar organization, histoimmuno-staining images.
  • Molecular
    RNA expression measured via PatchSeq.

Highlights

Recently we've updated our data  server with both electrophysiological and morphology data!

~270 electrophysiological recordings

Stimulus protocols: long (1s) and short (3ms) current pulse. Shown above are the waveforms of the rheobase action potential.

10 morphological reconstructions

Imaging was performed at 60x magnification with a Leica confocal microscope. Reconstructions completed with Neurolucida.
