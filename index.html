Primate Cell Type Database

Intracellular recordings of non-human primates (NHP) cells sampled from the lateral prefrontal cortex.

patch clamp recordings for 2 protocols: 1 second current pulse & 3 millisecond pulse. Morphology obtained by biocytin injections and confocal imaging at 63x magnification.

Ex vivo data 

We have a suite of experimental data generated recently in the lab including: electrophysiology, morphology, and transcriptomics.

  • Electrophysiology
    Patch clamp recordings for various stimulation protocols (i.e., long current pulse, 3ms pulse, ramp, etc.).
  • Morphology
    3-D morphological reconstructions, laminar organization, histoimmuno-staining images.
  • Molecular
    RNA expression measured via PatchSeq.
